How to clip with AI Video Clipper

Creating clips has never been easier. Simply follow these steps and make AI video clipping as easy as 1-2-3.

1. Upload one or more video files or Enter a URL

Upload one or more video files or Enter a URL to AI Video Clipper.

2. Clip or Trim with AI

Customize your video clips effortlessly with AI Video Clipper: Click the 'Start Snipping' button to begin scene extraction or click 'Cancel' to stop the process. Specify scene extraction frames per second as needed.

3. Download All

Once you finish trimming with AI Video Clipper, simply click 'Download All' to save all the scene extracted.
